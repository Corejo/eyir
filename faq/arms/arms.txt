**__FAQ__**

**Q:** What is the best Covenant?
**A:** At this moment, **Venthyr** is the best covenant for raiding. However **Night Fae** and **Kyrian** are still very much viable in raids, and much better for AoE and M+. 

**Q:** What are the best Potency Conduits?
**A:** Single Target: Ashen Juggernaut (Venthyr) > Mortal Combo ~= > Ashen Juggernaut (Non-Venthyr) > Covenant Conduits
       Aoe/M+: Crash the Ramparts > Piercing Verdict (Kyrian) = Destructive Reverberations (Nightfae) > Merciless Bonegrinder > Veterans Rpute (Necrolord) Mortal Combo > Rest

**Q:** What are the best Soulbindings?
**A:** Venthyr: Nadjita ~= Theotar > Draven
       Night Fae: Niya > Korayn > Dreamweaver
       Kyrian: Pelagos > Forgelite > Kleia
       Necrolord: Bonesmith > Plague Deviser > Emeni

**Q:** What are the best Legendary Effects and what gear slot should they be crafted on?
**A:** Single Target: Signet of Tormented Kings >  Unhinged > Enduring Blow > Battlelord > Explolter 
       AoE: Signet of Tormented Kings > Seismic Reverberation > Unhinged > Rest
       Legendaries should be crafted on gear with the most strength. This can vary from person to person.

**Q:** What is the general stat priority?
**A:** Strength > Critical Strike > Haste to 20% > Mastery > Versatility > Haste
__THIS CAN VARY FROM CHARACTER TO CHARACTER PLEASE SIM FOR MORE ACCURATE WEIGHTS.__
